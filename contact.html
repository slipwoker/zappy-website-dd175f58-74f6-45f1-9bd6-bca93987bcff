Frequently Asked Questions

Common inquiries before you reach out

What types of events does Hibernian Hall host?

From intimate bridal and baby showers to grand weddings, lively birthday parties, and polished corporate events — our elegant Irish catering venue is designed to host a wide range of celebrations with warmth and sophistication.

Do you offer planning support for our celebration?

Absolutely. Our dedicated team works closely with you to curate every detail, from bespoke menus and floral arrangements to event flow and entertainment, ensuring a seamless and memorable experience.

What is the atmosphere like at the venue?

Hibernian Hall blends timeless Irish charm with modern elegance. Expect warm ambient lighting, rich textures, and an inviting atmosphere that feels both ceremonial and comfortably hospitable.

How far in advance should we inquire?

We recommend reaching out as early as possible, especially for popular dates. Many couples and event hosts secure their booking 6–12 months in advance, but we always welcome last-minute inquiries and will do our best to accommodate.
